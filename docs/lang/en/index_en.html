How it works

For each frontline employee a personal page is created.
It contains contact information and photo.
This information can be downloaded and saved
as new contact in smatphone right from this page.
Link to this page can be shared through different touchpoints:

Placement of the QR code
at the workplace

Messaging
via messenger or SMS

Electronic employee
ID card

Printed primary
accounting documents
(purchse order or
sales contract)

Using this page customers will be able to rate the employee's performance.

For customers who have given a low rating from 1 to 3 it is suggested to write their feedback in a form on site. This allows you to quickly resolve the issue and deal with bad reviews before they are posted somewhere else.

Customers who rate
from 4 to 5 are offered to visit mapping services, where they can leave a positive review and rating. We recommend implementing KPIs in the motivation system for frontline employees depending on reviews left.

After the client leaves a rating, the data is sent to responsible employees by Telegram or E-mail

Also, all the statistics for each employee, along with their ratings, are automatically collected in a Google Spreadsheet.
